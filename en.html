New Discord BOT for Warzone Streamers!

Would you like to bring more utility and engagement to your Discord server?

Introducing WZL, a bot created especially for Call of Duty: Warzone streamers, with a simple and powerful function:

  • 🛠️ Share your loadouts with your community quickly and in an organized way.
  • 🎮 Your followers can easily view and copy your favorite classes directly from Discord.

Whether you're looking to make your server more interactive or just want to share your setups more easily, WZL is the perfect tool for you.

Bot Examples:

/add_load     ➕  Add a new loadout
/del_load     ❌  Delete a loadout
/edit_load    ✏️  Edit a loadout (use "Optic: NO" to remove the sight)
/loadouts     📦  Show available loadouts
/setbot       📍  Use commands only in a specific channel
/unsetbot     🔄  Allow bot usage in all channels again
