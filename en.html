Introducing WZL, a bot created especially for Call of Duty: Warzone streamers, with a simple and powerful function:

Bot Examples:

Available Commands:

/add_load     Add a new loadout
/del_load     Delete a loadout
/edit_load    Edit a loadout (use "Optic: NO" to remove the sight)
/loadouts     Show available loadouts
/setbot       Set a specific channel for bot commands only
/unsetbot     Remove the channel restriction
/info         View bot information
